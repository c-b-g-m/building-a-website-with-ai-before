About Bayou Brew Coffee

A passionately curated coffee experience in the heart of the Heights.

"We believe that every cup of coffee is an opportunity to elevate your day. That's why we are committed to delivering only the finest, most artisanally crafted experience to our valued community." — Bayou Brew Founders

Our Story

Bayou Brew Coffee was born in 2018 from a passion for community and a deep appreciation for the craftsmanship of coffee. Our founders, both Houston natives, set out to create a space where neighbors could gather, connect, and experience the very best in artisanal coffee.

Located in the vibrant Houston Heights, our cozy café has become a beloved gathering place for locals seeking a moment of warmth, connection, and elevated taste.

Our Approach

Every bean we serve is hand-selected by our dedicated team. We work directly with small-batch roasters who share our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the art of coffee. Our menu is intentionally curated — we'd rather do a few things exceptionally well than try to be everything to everyone.

Our Community

We are proud to be more than just a coffee shop. We host monthly tasting events, latte art demonstrations, and welcome our regulars as family. Three visits and you'll be on the invite list — that's how we operate.
